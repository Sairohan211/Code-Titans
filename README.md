🚀 What is VitaScan?

Ever opened a medical report and felt lost?

VitaScan AI Agent** transforms complex lab reports into:

* 📊 Simple, human-friendly explanations
* 🚨 Clear priority health alerts
* 🍱 Personalized food suggestions (based on your country)
* 🏃 Practical daily lifestyle guidance
* 🎙️ Voice-based AI assistant for interactive help

---

⚡ Why it stands out

* 🧠 AI that explains like a human, not a textbook
* 🌍 Smart, country-aware recommendations
* 🎯 Focus on what actually matters (priority alerts)
* 🎙️ Listen to your health insights (Murf AI voice)
* 💬 Interactive assistant with quick prompts
